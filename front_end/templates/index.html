CREDIT SCORING SYSTEM

PROJECT COME FROM
In light of the US Federal Reserve's ongoing interest rate hikes, the financial market and lending environment have been significantly affected. As borrowing costs rise, customers face increasing repayment pressure, leading to a gradual rise in credit default risks. Therefore, developing a precise credit scoring model becomes crucial for financial institutions to evaluate risk and navigate uncertain market fluctuations. Effective risk assessment and management are paramount in this dynamic business landscape.
=================================
TARGET FEATURES
Develop an artificial intelligence model capable of precisely assessing an individual's credit risk for the purpose of predicting their credit score. Utilize a substantial dataset from historical records, along with personal financial particulars and pertinent attributes, to train the model. This endeavor serves to amplify the accuracy and prognostic proficiency of credit scoring mechanisms.
=================================
FUNCTIONAL OVERVIEW
The primary objective of this model is to conduct a thorough analysis and data processing utilizing the applicant's personal information and financial status. Its predictive capabilities revolve around determining the applicant's ability to meet loan repayment obligations punctually. This sophisticated model facilitates enhanced credit risk evaluation by financial institutions during the loan application process. Through the application of data visualization techniques, it effectively elucidates the constituent factors that contribute to the scoring process, thereby rendering loan approval or rejection decisions more informed and rational.
